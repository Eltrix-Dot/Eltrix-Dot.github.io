Expert Protection Tips üß†

1. Kill Your Metadata: Every photo you upload contains "EXIF data" (GPS coordinates, camera model). Use an EXIF cleaner before posting images online.
2. DNS over HTTPS (DoH): Even with a VPN, your DNS queries can leak. Enable "Secure DNS" in your browser settings to prevent your ISP from seeing which domains you visit.
3. The 2FA Trap: Never use SMS for 2FA. Hackers can "SIM-swap" you. Use 2FA apps like Google Authenticator or Ente Auth instead.
